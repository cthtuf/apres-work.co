Services

All your needs in one place.

Coaching
The essentials - a four way training regime to help our campers accelerate and sustain their progression: starting on trampoline, moving onto an airbag, taking it to the snow and then recovering physically and mentally through yoga.

Media
Our media crew is here to make your experience unique and memorable. We have a dedicated photographer, and a professional video operator to capture every moment of your camp experience.

Entertainment
We have a calendar packed-full with activities! When we are not training you will be doing anything from golf and mountain biking to traveling and partying, we got it all. We even get a chance to go to Côte d'Azur and lie around on the beach sipping cocktails.

Organization
Simply put - you do not have to worry about a thing, it has all been taken care of in advance. All you have to do is come. You can also customise your camp stay by modifying your program, we can create a custom package just for you.

How it works

A short summary of the camp

apres-work.co camp

ultimate summer camp

This summer, we’re bringing skiers and snowboarders to the French Alps to ride, progress, and party with Apres-Work Camps. We’ve taken care of every part of your summer ski vacation, it’s up to you to choose how you want to live it (pick and choose).

Les Deux Alpes is a year-round ski resort tucked away in the Southern Alps. During the summer, freestyle skiers and snowboarders take over the glacier, spending the season jumping and sliding at 3600 meters. Apres-Work is here to (work on progression). If you want to train hard this summer or spend some time relaxing in the sun and the snow, Apres-Work has got it covered.

Up on the glacier, you can train with our freestyle coaches. We offer group lessons of up to 8 skiers or snowboarders where you can train with our coaches, all depending on your freestyle goals. Both group and private lessons are offered and coached by our qualified instructors who have worked around the world: from Japan to Australia, New Zealand to USA.
